Vehicle Diagnostics

Development of diagnostic modules in i14230 and GmLanDia protocol on CAN medium

Project involves development of tester services for Suzuki based on KWP2000 protocol and tester services for ISUZU on GmLanDia protocol. The services are as follows 1) Reading of engine dynamic data from engine Electronic Control Unit (ECU). 2)Writing of static data such as vehicle identification number , programming date to the emulated EEPROM 3)Controlling of input and output actuators. 4)Reading of freeze frame data.

  • Date: July 2011
  • Client:SUZUKI
  • Category:Tester Diagnostics
